Welcome to the Gender Recognition WebApp

Our web application utilizes state-of-the-art machine learning techniques to analyze facial images and predict gender with remarkable accuracy.

How Our Model Works

Our system begins with face detection using OpenCVâ€™s Haar Cascade classifier. Once a face is detected, it undergoes a series of preprocessing steps: normalization, resizing, and flattening. Then, we subtract the mean face and use Principal Component Analysis (PCA) for dimensionality reduction. Finally, the processed image is fed into an SVM classifier to predict the gender with a corresponding confidence score. This robust pipeline ensures accurate and efficient gender classification even under varying conditions.

The entire process is optimized to handle both static images and videos, making it a versatile solution for various applications.
